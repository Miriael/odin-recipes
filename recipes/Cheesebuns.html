Cheesebuns Recipe

A delicious appetizer that can also be used as burger buns!

  • 220 g Mozzarella Grated
  • 3 Eggs Large
  • 5 g Baking powder
  • 20 Psyllium Husk Powder
  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl, mix well
  2. Preheat oven to 190C
  3. Allow to stand for 15-20 mins
  4. Divide dough into 4, place on baking sheet
  5. Bake at 190C for 15-20 mins until golden brown
  6. Allow to cool & serve
