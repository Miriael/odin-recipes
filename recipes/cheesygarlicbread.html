Cheesy Garlic Bread Recipe

A tasty italian appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 250g Mozarella grated
  • 85g Almond flour
  • 2tbsp Cream cheese full fat
  • 1tbsp Garlic crushed
  • 1tsp Baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Egg medium

Steps

  1. In a microwaveable bowl, combine all ingredients except the egg, mix well.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute on high
  3. Stir well & microwave on high for another 30 seconds.
  4. Add the egg, mix gently to make a cheesy dough.
  5. Place onto a baking tray, form into a loaf shape.
  6. Cut slices into the dough.
  7. Bake at 220C for 15 mins.
